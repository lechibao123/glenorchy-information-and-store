How To Find Us

It is easy to escape the high adrenline and action packed down of queenstown and find yourself immeresd in the natural surrounding of Glenorchy’s hight country.

Distance

Glenorchy is only one hour scenic drive from Queenstown Airport.

Transport To And From Glenorchy

Glenorchy information center located at Glenorchy Hotel can organise transport from queenstown to Glenorchy and then onto one of the multi day walking tracks in the area. After hours transport can be arranged. For more information on the walking tracks click here. Book now – Contact us for transport information and bookings.

All Roads To Glenorchy via Queenstown

You have to travel through Queenstown to reach Glenorchy, which will give you an excellent opportunity to look round the shop and resturants in the center,or to take a trip out to the gold rush town to Arrowston

New Zealand Accomodation & Travel Plans

During the main travel season (december to april) it is imperative that you are organise your accomodation and main activities prior to arriving, this will ensure your travel plans are not disrupted and will avoid disappointment. Booking and reservations can be made at Glenorchy Information Center located at Glenorchy Hotel.

To help these plans we can recommend and organise your transpoort, accommodation and activities. We Recommend you contact us with an outline of your proposed journey.
