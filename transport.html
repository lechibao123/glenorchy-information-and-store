Transport

Getting Around
Glenorchy, NZ

Download the track transport timetable here.

Glenorchy information center located at Glenorchy Hotel can organise transport from Queenstown to Glenorchy and then onto one of the multi day walking tracks in the area. After hours transport can be arranged.

All track transport leaves from the Glenorchy Hotel and Glenorchy NZ information center General store located on the main street of Glenorchy. All regular bus service to and from Queenston depart from the Glenorchy information Center located at the Glenorchy Hotel then leave for the various tracks from the same information Center.
